Our Specialisations

Focused wet works specialists — Bricklaying, Plastering, Skimming, Ceilings and Partitions.

Our Approach

Specialists, Not Generalists

We deliberately focus on what we do best. By specialising in wet works, we deliver a higher standard than a general contractor spreading themselves thin. Main contractors choose Rozvi because they know exactly what they will get — precision, reliability, and a clean handover.

Core Services

Our Five Wet Works Disciplines

🧱
01

Bricklaying

Structural and face brickwork executed to exacting standards. Our bricklayers are experienced across residential apartment blocks, commercial buildings, and mixed-use developments. We lay to datum, manage our own setting out, and maintain consistent joint profiles throughout.

Structural brickwork and blockwork
Face brickwork and feature walls
Infill and repair brickwork

02

Plastering

Sand and cement plastering for interior and exterior applications. We achieve straight, plumb, and level surfaces that form the foundation for all subsequent finishes. Our teams work efficiently to keep pace with busy construction programmes without compromising on quality.

Rough plaster (sand & cement)
Smooth plaster finishes
External render
Request a Quote
🌁
⭐
03

Skimming

Finishing skim coat applied over plaster or board to create a paint-ready surface. Our skimmers produce consistently flat, smooth results — ideal for high-specification residential and hospitality developments where the final finish must be flawless and blemish-free.

Skim coat over plaster
Skim over plasterboard
High-spec paint-ready finishes
Request a Quote
04

Ceilings

Suspended and direct-fixed ceiling installations for residential apartment blocks, hotels, offices, and healthcare facilities. We coordinate closely with mechanical and electrical trades to avoid clashes and delays — keeping your programme on track.

Suspended T-bar ceiling grids
Acoustic ceiling systems
Fire-rated ceiling assemblies

Dry-wall Partitions

Interior partition wall systems installed to drawing and specification. We handle standard, acoustic, and fire-rated configurations for office fit-outs, apartment developments, hotels, and healthcare environments. Straight, plumb, and ready for finish on schedule.

Standard and acoustic partitions
Fire-rated partition systems
Shaft walls and service enclosures
