Our Story

Building South Africa From the Ground Up

The Rozvi Construction Company (Pty) Ltd was founded in 2016 with a clear vision: to deliver world-class construction and renovation services while contributing meaningfully to South Africa's transformation agenda.

Led by owner Mr. Nyasha Machokoro, we are a 100% Black Owned company and proud Level 1 B-BBEE contributor operating across Cape Town and Johannesburg. Since our founding, we have successfully completed projects across the residential, commercial, and institutional sectors.

Our approach combines technical expertise with a genuine commitment to sustainable development, community upliftment, and the highest ethical standards in the industry.
