Who We Are

Wet Works Specialists Since 2016

The Rozvi Construction Company (Pty) Ltd was established in 2016 with a focused mission — to be the most reliable and skilled wet works subcontractor in South Africa.

We are a 100% Black Owned, Level 1 B-BBEE company operating as specialist subcontractors to main contractors across Gauteng and the Western Cape. Our strength lies in our specialisation: Bricklaying, Plastering, Skimming, Ceilings and Partitions.

We understand the pressures main contractors face. Our role is to make your programme easier — arriving prepared, working clean, and delivering quality finishes that hold up to the highest scrutiny.

Our Role

We Are Subcontractors — And We Take That Seriously

We work for and alongside main contractors who entrust us with key scopes of work on their projects. We recognise that our performance reflects directly on them — and we take that responsibility seriously.

Our commitment is simple: show up ready, work to specification, coordinate effectively, and hand over a finish that makes the whole project look good. We are not here to compete with our principals — we are here to make them shine.
